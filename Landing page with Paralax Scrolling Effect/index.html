    We’re the tea that
    pops.

    Intentionally brewed to be light, Minna makes the good stuff without all the… well… not so good stuff. It’s actually easier this way. Using only organic and non-gmo ingredients, with no sugars or added sweeteners, you can feel good about what we’re brewing. Refreshing, right?

    Peach Yuzu Green Tea

    Remember that bottled peach tea that you grew up with? Remember all the added sugar? And that “tea” was the third or fourth item in the ingredient listing?

    Well, we upgraded this classic for the adult-you, the MINNA way. Using only Fair Trade organic green tea, we balanced sweet organic peach and tart organic yuzu flavors…perfectly.

    With no added sugar or sweeteners and only 40mg of caffeine, your adult-self will thank you

    Minna advocates for inclusion.

    We wanted to create a better sparkling beverage, inside and out. Minna donates 1% of sales to non-profits advocating for inclusion.

    The first organization that we are partnering with is Help Refugees and Choose Love. Help Refugees has over 70 projects in Europe and the Middle East that provide emergency aid and long term solutions for people seeking refuge. Choose Love is the world’s first e-commerce store where all products purchased are donated directly to refugees in need.

    As we grow, we plan to diversify the use of funds to non-profits focused on helping those communities still fighting for equality.
